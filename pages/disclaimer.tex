\textbf{Use of Generative AI Tools.}
In accordance with the chair's guideline on declaring the use of generative AI
for writing and implementation assistance, the following tools were used in the
preparation of this thesis. \textit{Claude Code} (Anthropic) was used for parts
of the implementation in the experiment repository
(\url{https://github.com/ndtrung00/agentic-ai-for-clm}) - scaffolding, code
refactoring, and debugging support. \textit{Claude} (Anthropic, desktop
application) was used for brainstorming research framings, drafting and
editing prose, and proofreading. The author reviewed and edited every passage
of text and every line of committed code; all factual claims, citations,
statistical results, and experimental conclusions were cross-validated against
primary sources and the underlying experiment artefacts before inclusion. The
author remains responsible for the contents of this thesis.
